Any Bell class is more about the process and critical thinking than anything else. If you are honest about how you work and come into the class willing to put the grade in the back of your mind to focus on content, you will do well and have a great experience. The bottom line is, the class is fun, and you will learn tons if you don't get too 'Lowell' about it. But since I know that's why you're here... If you are a freshman taking him for World, the class is a guaranteed A and will probably be your most enjoyable class. If you are taking him for an AP, you will be incredibly prepared for the AP test because not only will you know the format; you will know the content like the back of your hand (assuming you are an engaged student in class). More or less, for AP's the class is a B for average effort, you have to work for an A. As a whole, he doesn't give a lot of homework, but you should make sure to put in the effort when he does.
