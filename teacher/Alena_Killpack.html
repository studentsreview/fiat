Ms. Killpack is a very nice teacher that is accommodating to students. I have her for AP Bio. Her class and Ms. Christensen's class are basically in the same material. Her tests are not overly hard, there's tests on Mondays that consist of around 12-14 MCQ and 1 FRQ. She will curve tests, and her class is not at all as long as you keep up with the material consistently. Part of the grade is participation, which is done every 2 weeks. You only have to participate twice, by either asking questions, passing out graded papers, or being the person tracking the participation. Very little homework. She gives out study guides for the test, but the study guides are fully optional and will not make your grade go down if you don't. She is not a harsh grader,and I really recommend her as a teacher.

She also gives extra credit if you go to LSRP every Tuesday and Thursday. Overall you don't learn as much as others I think but I recommend her if you want an easier and funner class.
