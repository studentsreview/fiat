Dance 3

Pre-Fall 2014 - Spring 2021

Visual Performing Arts
Spring 2021
Prerequisites: None

A-G: F-VPA

Length: Yearlong

Description: Dance 3 is for students who wish to excel in the art of dance through exposure to advanced techniques. Students have opportunities to perform student and professional choreographed works in front of an audience. Genres covered include contemporary, ballet, jazz, tap, and cultural/traditional dance forms. While learning these various techniques, students develop an advanced level of vocabulary and movement to help them define their steps at a professional pace and level in both improvisational work and choreography.
